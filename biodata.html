BIODATA


My name is Nur Izzati binti Mohd Abdul Malek. My friends and family usually call me izzati. This year I am 20 years old. I was born and also live in Terengganu, Malaysia. Since birth I have lived in Terengganu until now. So is my mother my father. They were both born and live in Terengganu. My father is self-employed while my mother works as a teacher. I am the second child of five siblings.I have one brother, two younger sisters and a younger brother. They are also like me born and raised in Terengganu. I can't tell if I'm an introvert or an extrovert because my nature is not fixed. Sometimes I like a quiet atmosphere sometimes I like a noisy atmosphere. There is a time when I like to be alone, silent and I don't like bothered. But sometimes I like to be in a crowd, very active, get bored quickly if I don't talk or do anything.
